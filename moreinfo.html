Dream Catch is a device that is worn at night while the consumer sleeps.

If they have a dream during their sleep, the Dream Catch device captures this

informtion.This information is then transmitted through to the Dream Catch

database and to your account where users log onto their account in order to

find out about these dreams that occured in their sleep.

Users are also able to take notes about their day, to log their

feelings, worries, and what's been on their mind in order to

determine if these influence or related to their dreams

Without Dream Catch, you'll never know what you dreamed about!

Be curious and learn how your day may influence your dreams.
