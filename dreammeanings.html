Animals

Dreaming of animals can mean you have a connection to nature and survival.

Being chased by one suggests you're holding back from something.

Being Chased

Dreaming about being chased can mean that you are feeling threatened.

It can also mean you are avoiding an issue you need to sort out

Falling

Dreaming about falling is quite common. It usually means we are feeling

anxious about something or like we have failed

Money

Dreaming about money means you are feeling self-worth

It may mean that you are going to have some changes occur in your life

Teeth Falling Out

Dreaming about teeth is also common and means you are feeling fear

This is usually fear of getting older or fear of feeling unnatractive
